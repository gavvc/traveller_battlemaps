This archive presents the Adventure Class Geomorph tiles in PNG format with transparent floor backgrounds. This allows you to overlay the images on a square or hexagon grid or no grid at all (baseplates with pre-rendered grids are provided in the archive. Or you can toggle the grid, as I did in my sample maps. These tiles are scaled to 60 pixels per 1.5 meter / 5 foot square (which I sometimes refer to as 60 dpi). This conveniently works out to 1 pixel = 1 inch, making a 1 yard hex 36 pixels across. The High Resolution version of the files 5x larger (300 pixels per square, 180 pixels per hex).
